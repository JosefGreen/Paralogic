## Current Novelty Hypothesis

The strongest current novelty hypothesis is not "a new logic of
contradiction." It is a Lean-first, non-collapse workbench that separates
formal diagnosis, evaluator acceptance, normative bridge warrants, repair
stages, empirical protocols, finite witnesses, and proof-check traces.

This hypothesis remains unconfirmed until a full related-work review and
external expert review occur.
