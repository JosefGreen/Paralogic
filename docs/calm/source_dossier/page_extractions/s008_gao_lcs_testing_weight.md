## Candidate Bounded Claim

The LCS case can support the claim that several partially true statements about
innovation, modularity, deployment, and testing may still fail to compose into
an adequately warranted whole-mission claim unless configuration, operating
environment, sustainment exposure, technical margin, and acquisition timing are
kept explicit.

## Counter-Evidence Required

- Later Navy, GAO, CRS, and DOT&E evidence on LCS deployments, mission
  packages, survivability trials, and fleet changes.
- Evidence of implemented weight-management improvements.
- Evidence of mission areas where LCS produced useful operational value.
- Currentness note separating 2014 acquisition risk from later fleet history.

## Manuscript Boundary

This extraction is not enough for final manuscript use. It can support a
bounded CALM claim about evidence composition and acquisition timing, but it
must be paired with newer evidence before any current LCS statement appears in
the book.
