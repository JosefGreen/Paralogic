## Candidate Bounded Claim

The F-35 case can support the claim that a system may be real, advanced, and
formally moved to a new production phase while still carrying separate bounded
claims about delivery performance, software/hardware modernization, supplier
risk, thermal-management decisions, and lifecycle burden.

## Counter-Evidence Required

- DOD/program office responses in the same report.
- Later GAO or DOT&E updates on TR-3, Block 4, delivery performance, and
  mission-capable rates.
- Evidence of improvements or mitigations after the report period.
- Operational-use evidence that prevents the chapter from implying platform
  uselessness.

## Manuscript Boundary

This extraction is not enough for final manuscript use. It can seed a chapter
claim, but the final paragraph must survive counter-evidence review and must
avoid the false collapse from sustainment risk to aircraft failure.
