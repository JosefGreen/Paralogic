## Verified Public Anchor

The NIST CSRC page identifies SP 800-37 Rev. 2 as a December 2018 Joint Task
Force publication. Its abstract describes the RMF as a disciplined, structured,
flexible process for managing security and privacy risk, including
categorization, control selection, implementation, assessment, authorization,
and continuous monitoring. The page links to the DOI and download URL.

## Bounded Claims This Source May Support After Page Extraction

1. RMF provides a structured process for security and privacy risk management.
2. Authorization and monitoring are part of a lifecycle process, not one-time
   proof of mission safety.
3. CALM can attach to RMF/ATO artifacts by clarifying what an authorization
   does and does not allow other actors to infer.
