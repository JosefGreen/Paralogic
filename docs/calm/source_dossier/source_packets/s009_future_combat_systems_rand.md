## Bounded Claims This Source May Support After Page Extraction

1. FCS is a strong case for studying how large system-of-systems acquisition
   efforts can accumulate integration, requirements, cost, schedule, and
   technology-risk burdens.
2. Wargaming, concept development, and technical assessment can generate useful
   insight, but they must not be treated as validation without preserved
   assumptions and technical evidence.
3. Whole-system capability claims require attention to how component
   requirements interoperate and how assumptions travel across concept,
   acquisition, and operational communities.
